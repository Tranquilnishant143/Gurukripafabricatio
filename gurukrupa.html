Our Story

Mumbai's Trusted Glass, Aluminium & Interior Experts

Gurukrupa Fabrication is your trusted specialist for all types of sliding windows, glass works, electric works, and all interior work in Dahisar, Mumbai. We design, supply, and install high-quality aluminium sliding windows, glass partitions, glass panels, and complete interior solutions for residential and commercial spaces.

Our work is known for precision fitting, clean finishing, and durable materials that stand up to Mumbai's climate. Whether you need a single window replaced or a full interior fitout done, we handle every project with care and craftsmanship.

  • Free site visit and measurement for every project
  • Residential & commercial work accepted
  • Quality aluminium profiles built for Mumbai's monsoon
  • Complete electric wiring, switchboard & interior work
  • Honest pricing — full estimate before work begins
